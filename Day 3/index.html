The University of Karachi is a public research university located in Karachi, Sindh, Pakistan. It is one of the oldest universities in Pakistan being established as a federal university in 1951 under the leadership of Oxford trained Dr. ABA Haleem. The university spans over 1,279 acres of land, situated 12 km away from the city center of Karachi.
